Kraft Dinner

Description

Kraft Dinner is a canadian brand of mac & cheese. It tastes alright and is easy to make

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Box of KD
  • Heat source

Steps

  1. Boil Water in a pot
  2. Put noodles in pot
  3. Wait for noodles to soften
  4. Drain water from pot
  5. Add cheese packet and mix
  6. enjoy
